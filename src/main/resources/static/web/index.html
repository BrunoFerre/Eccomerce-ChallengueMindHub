About Us

The beginning of everything

Our store started with the purpose of giving happiness to our customers through typical South American drinks. We are a group of 4 entrepreneurs between Argentina, Chile and Colombia who want to bring to the whole world the American culture and drinks that have existed for decades, bringing one and a thousand stories to the whole world.

Coffee

One of the best known beverages worldwide is coffee, this beverage known for its diverse preparations could not be missing in our catalog. With its peculiar aroma we want to captivate you and give you the pleasure of tasting every drop of quality.

Mate

Mate is a drink with South American origins that has been around for decades. Since its discovery it has been a predominant drink in Argentina, Uruguay and Paraguay. It is obtained from the mate plant and its main grower is Argentina.
